About Us

Creating Elegance and Durability: Wooden Creations by Custom Shed LTD

With over 12 years of experience in Ireland and across Europe, "Custom Shed" has established itself as a leader in crafting modern wooden structures.

Our expertise lies in creating wooden garden sheds, summer houses, and designer flower beds. We use high-quality treated wood to ensure durability and an exquisite appearance of our products. We guarantee that each project combines a unique style with reliability.
